Research Focus

What I Investigate

01

Natural Language Processing

Parsing, understanding, and generating human language — from cross-lingual transfer to semantic retrieval at scale.

02

Knowledge Graphs & QA

Dynamic conditional gating and contrastive learning for question answering over incomplete and heterogeneous knowledge bases.

03

Large Language Models

Retrieval-augmented generation, knowledge distillation, and mixture-of-experts architectures for advanced reasoning tasks.

04

Information Retrieval

Dense passage retrieval and scaled momentum contrastive learning to connect questions with precise, contextual answers.

05

Agent-based Modeling

Simulating heterogeneous traffic and complex adaptive systems to study emergent real-world phenomena.

06

Computer Vision

Deep learning for visual understanding.
